Recipe Title

Chicken Adobo recipe

Chicken Adobo is a traditional filipino recipe consisting of simmered onions, fried garlic, and tender, slow-cooked chicken covered in a delicious soy-based sauce. Typically served over rice with a boiled egg as garnish, this easy to make recipe is a definite crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • yellow onion
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar of choice (apple cider or white vinegar)
  • rice
  1. slice onion
  2. debone and cut up chicken thighs
  3. preheat pan
  4. pour 1 tbsp oil into pan
  5. saute onions, when translucent remove from pan, set aside
  6. brown chicken thighs
  7. combine all Ingredients and cook until tender
