Hiyayakko recipe

Hiyayakko or Japanese Cold Tofu is a perfect appetizer or side dish that you can whip up instantly! Youâ€˜ll love how smooth and silky it tastes. For many fun variations, feel free to switch up the toppings. Serve the tofu on a hot summer day and enjoy it with a cold beer

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1sp sesame oil
  • chopped scallions, for garnish
  • 200g cooked rice
  1. cut silken tofu into large cubes, aprox. 2 inch
  2. combine soy, sesame oil, and sugar--mix
  3. pour over tofu
  4. garnish with chopped scallions
